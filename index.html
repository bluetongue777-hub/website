FAQs

Common Questions

What's the difference between Level 4 and Level 5?

Level 4 is the standard finish — smooth and ready for most paint and wallpaper jobs. Level 5 adds an extra skim coat for the highest-quality finish, ideal under critical lighting or with gloss paints.

How long after stopping can I paint?

You can paint straight away — no waiting required.

How long does a typical room take?

A standard room takes around 4–5 days from start to finish. Larger jobs and new builds we'll quote with a clear timeline.

Do you do small repairs?

Absolutely — no job too small. Cracks, holes, water damage, dings from moving furniture — if it's a wall or ceiling, we can patch it.

What areas do you cover?

Dunedin and surrounds — including Mosgiel, Brockville, St Kilda, St Clair, Andersons Bay, Green Island. We can also travel further out of Dunedin for the right job — just get in touch.
